Section 21 - Cascading Approval 

Configuring a series of approvals, achieved via "reporting structure" and "authority matrix"

Cascading approval step:
	Simplifies the configuration of multiple approvals, can be base on Authority matrix or Reporting structure. 

Authority matrix 
	Uses a decision table to identify each party in the approval process as well as the conditions that determine when each party must provide approval.

Reporting Structure
	Used to gather approval if they follow a strict heirachal structure.
